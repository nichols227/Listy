Dear rational, utility-maximizing consumer

Welcome to Listy, and thank you for signing up for the Listy list! We will be sending you [occasional] updates on the service’s development over the upcoming weeks. The official launch of the service will take place this summer of 2016, so we know what you’re thinking: Why should I stay on this list?

Deals. Deals out the wazoo and back up it again. That’s why.

We know you signed up for this mailing list because you are highly interested in using a service that guarantees you The. Lowest. Price. for grocery delivery, so why not save more money while you’re at it?

By signing up for this list, you have already marked yourself as one of our first users, giving yourself a shot at an array of deals, so thank you for helping us help you.

So what is Listy? Listy is the world’s first price comparison tool for grocery delivery. Think of Listy like Kayak but for grocery delivery. Kayak aggregates data from different airlines and shows the lowest possible cost. Wouldn’t it be great if supermarkets and online vendors had similar systems? It would. That’s why we’re here.

So stay tuned for updates and the wazoo-deals. In the meantime, please feel free to send the link www.trylisty.com to your friends so that they can sign up, too!
